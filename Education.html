Education

I laid the foundation for my education, completing my studies up to the 7th grade with a focus on building strong fundamentals and a love for learning.

From 8th to 12th grade, my educational journey took an unconventional turn as I engaged in self-directed learning at Aksharavanam, a distinguished research and educational center. Guided by the esteemed SPR, I delved into a holistic approach to education, fostering not just knowledge acquisition but also critical thinking and research skills

Currently, I am enrolled in the first year of the Computer Human Interaction Design (CHD) program at the International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Hyderabad. This exciting phase is a result of my successful performance in the Undergraduate Entrance Examination (UGEE), marking the beginning of my journey into the realms of technology, design, and user-centric experiences.
